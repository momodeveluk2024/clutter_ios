How it works

Three steps, then it disappears.

The best tracker is the one you forget you're using. NV is designed to fade into your routine within a week.

STEP 01

Set your reference

Tell NV your sex and life stage once. We use NIH-published DRI values to calibrate the gauges.

STEP 02

Log what you eat

Search 40,000+ foods or scan a barcode. Pick a serving size, hit save. That's the whole interaction.

STEP 03

Watch the gaps close

The dashboard shows which nutrients you keep missing — usually D, B12 or Mg — so you can adjust naturally.
